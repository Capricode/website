Swiss-O-Week 2021

Official companion app for multiple days orienteering event Swiss-O-Week 2021, that takes place in Arosa.

The features include push notifications, browsing information about the stages and the event itself. You can also add and follow all your friends, see start times for each stage day and check results in the real time.

Application was written using Xamarin.Forms for iOS and Android devices. It supports two languages: German and English. Updating of the data was handled using ReactiveUI.

MyOApp

One place to search and browse all orienteering events happening in Switzerland. Be always up-to-date with what’s happening nearby.

You can pick regions and favorite events to see them on your dashboard. After selecting an event, you gain access to it’s detailed information on how to get there, results, startlists, maps and more.

Application was written using Xamarin.Forms for iOS and Android devices. The app is available in different languages: Swiss German, German, French, English and Polish. Change theme from bright to dark for better app experience.
